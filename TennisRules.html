Rules For Table Tennis:

  • You always serve at start and after a point.
  • To hit the ball, drag your mouse towards bottom and then go towards the ball.
  • A serve must bounce first before the receiving player can return it.
  • If the ball bounces 2 times or more on your side of table, it's your opponent's point.
  • If the ball bounces 2 times or more on your opponent's side of table, it's your point.
